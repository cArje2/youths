SOMESHA KIJANA

Empowering a youths education, in Kibra community.

Every child deserves a chance to dream, to grow, and to thrive. Yet, in the heart of the Kibra community, six bright and determined young lives hang in the balance. They dream of stepping into classrooms, holding books instead of burdens, and building futures filled with possibility—but they cannot do it alone. This is where the Somesha Kijana Program comes in. At the Wezesha Youth Initiative, we are dedicated to giving these young people the opportunity to rewrite their stories. Through this program, we aim to provide access to education and skills training for youth who have faced insurmountable challenges. We are reaching out to you with a simple yet powerful request: partner with us to change lives. With your support, we can ensure these six youths return to school by September 2025, giving them the tools they need to unlock their potential and break free from the cycle of poverty.

#Somesha kijana 2025 program

The Somesha Kijana Program, an initiative by the Wezesha Youth Initiative, aims to empower underprivileged youth in the Kibra community by providing access to education and skills training. Set to kick off in September 2025, the program seeks to send at least six deserving youths back to school, offering them a chance to transform their lives and build brighter futures. Through the support of partners and well-wishers, we hope to break barriers and create lasting change in their lives.

About us

Wezesha Youth Initiative is a Community based Organization based in Kibra Community. Wezesha is a Swahili word which simply means to “Enable or to Empower.”Our Mission is To Empower Youths in general with skills and Information to better their lives. Our Key Objective is To Empower Youths in different aspects of life for them to be productive Members of the Society.

We invite you to walk this journey with us.
